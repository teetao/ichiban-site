Visa To China

Tourist (L) Visa

China Tourist Visa is issued to an individual who plans to travel to China for sightseeing or visiting family or friends or for other personal purposes.

Family Visit (L) Visa

Issued to an alien who comes to China for visiting family members/friends.

Business (F) Visa

Issued to an alien who comes to China for sightseeing.

Work (Z) Visa

Issued to the Department office or employment in China and their accompanying family members.

Study (X) Visa

Issued to students who will study in China for more than 180 days. An individual who plans to travel to China for study, short-term advanced studies or internship for a period less than 6 months shall apply for a China Business Visa (F Visa).
